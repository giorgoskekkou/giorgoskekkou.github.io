Giorgos Kekkou

I am a Computer Engineer passionate about bridging the gap between hardware and software. My main research interest is Computer Architecture, with a focus on CPU and GPU design. I enjoy working on projects and exploring new research directions to enrich my knowledge about the whole computer stack.
